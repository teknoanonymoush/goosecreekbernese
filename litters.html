Fun Facts

All of our breeding dogs are tested for Heart, Eyes, Hips, Elbows, Thyroid, Histiocytic Sarcoma, DM and VWd. We only breed once or twice a year, and have puppies available from select litters.

  • We have chose the Bernese Mountain Dog, because of their gentle disposition, loyalty, and devotion. As a family that seems to always be growing we just love this breed. I grew up always having dogs, so it seems fitting that I chose this amazing breed for my children enjoy and grow up with as well.
