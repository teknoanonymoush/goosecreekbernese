    About Us

    Goosecreek Bernese, situated in Rockglen SK, I'm a breeder of quality, CKC registered Berner puppies. Our dogs are all bred for health, temperament and to breed standard.

    All of our breeding dogs are tested for Heart, Eyes, Hips, Elbows, Thyroid, Histiocytic Sarcoma, DM and VWd. We only breed once or twice a year, and have puppies available from select litters.

    We have chose the Bernese Mountain Dog, because of their gentle, diposition, loyalty, and devotion. As a family that seems to always be growing we just love this breed. I grew up always having dogs. So it seems fitting that I have chosen this amazing breed for my children to enjoy and grow up with as well.

    My husband and I have lived on our beautiful ranch for the last nine years. Where we have welcomed with our three beautiful children. We run a large & cow/calf operation as well as grain operation alongside our Bernese.
